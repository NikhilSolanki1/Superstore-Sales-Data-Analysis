# Superstore-Sales-Data-Analysis
## Project Objective
To Contribute to the success of Business by utilising data analysis techniques, specifically focusing on time series analysis, to provide valuable insights and accurate sales forecasting

## Dataset used
- <a href="https://github.com/NikhilSolanki1/Superstore-Sales-Data-Analysis/blob/main/SuperStore_Sales_Dataset.csv">Super Store Dataset</a>

## Questions (KPIs)
1. Which Payment mode is used in different region?
2. Which region had the most sales?
3. In which year did the company had more sales?
4. What Products were mostly bought by customers?
5. What is the total Profit?
6. Forecast the sales for future 15 days
7. Which country will have most sale based on forecast?

-Dashboard Interaction <a href="https://github.com/NikhilSolanki1/Superstore-Sales-Data-Analysis/blob/main/DashBoard_Image.png">View Dasboard</a>

## Process
1. Cleaning Data and removing missing values and sorting data
2. Created varoius visual charts based on the questions asked.
3. Created slicer based on regions and created KPIs
4. Did forecasting using the Sales data and found out insights and future sales

## Insights
1. Found out that most regions used COD as their payment option but in western region people used online payment method most
2. The most selling product of Super Store is phones
3. People mostly buy office supplies in every region
4. Super Store had 2x increase in their sales in 2020
5. But they still had more profit in the month of December in 2019
6. Found out that California will be having most sales in next 15 days
7. Company will not have a great hike in 15 days but their is a decent hike in profit based on last 1 month
